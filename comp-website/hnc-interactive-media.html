Interactive Media

Level of Qualification: HNC
SCQF Level: 7

Ever wondered what opportunities are out thereto work creatively using digital technology? Do you want to work on a new video game title? Or develop websites for a living? Well, this course teaches you the fundamentals of working in this exciting industry to allow you to progress onto your specialised field of work.
Working on a number of projects you will develop the practical skills to work in a real environment. External live projects and exchange trips have become a feature of this course.

Further Information:
Visit this blog page for a full list of units covered: http://lecturerscott.wordpress.com/block-2/

Duration
One academic year.

Aim:
You will gain knowledge and expertise in subjects such as user interface design and web development, digital imaging for interactive design, 2D digital animation and audio visual techniques. You will also develop skills in responsive design, storyboarding and many other areas.

Entry:
2 Higher Grade passes in Graphics, Computing or Art & Design, or an NQ in Digital Media Computing, Graphic Design, Illustration and Animation, Computing or other relevant qualification.
Good basic IT skills are required as well as a level of self-discipline.
