Computing

Level of Qualification: HNC
SCQF Level: 7

This course which has recently been modernised by SQA, develops higher level skills and knowledge in software development and technical support, as well as developing core skills. It is suitable for individuals wanting to further their studies from NQ Computing or to develop a career in Computing or Information Technology.

Duration:
One academic year. You will need to attend College for 15 hours of variable, timetabled study per week, plus commit to undertake further self study of at least 6 hours per week.

Aim:
You will gain knowledge and expertise in subjects such as information technology, computer operating systems, programming, web development, application software, working within a project team and computer architecture. A final Graded Unit examination will assess the skills you have developed throughout the course.

Opportunities:
You will have the opportunity to complete a Microsoft MCITP qualification, recognised by local and national employers, enhancing your employment opportunities. There will also be the chance to meet potential employers.
Opportunities are available to study at College as an Associate of Edinburgh Napier University.

Entry:
1 Higher Grade pass (Grade C or above) plus 2 Standard Grade passes at Credit level, or an appropriate range of SQA units at Intermediate 2 and Higher level.
Mature candidates with relevant experience will be considered.
