Computing: Software Development

Level of Qualification: HND
SCQF Level: 8

This course will introduce you to a large array of software development skills which are required to compete in an expanding software workforce. You will learn about software engineering concepts, project management, and programming C++, web authoring, SQL, database design C# and Java.

Duration:
One academic year. You will need to attend College for 15 hours of variable, timetabled study per week, plus commit to undertake further self- study of at least 6 hours per week.

Aim:
You will gain knowledge and expertise in subjects such as: Object Oriented Programming; Object Oriented Analysis and Design; Data Structures; Testing Software; Developing Websites for Multiplatform Use; Introduction to Project Management; Mobile Technology; SQL: Introduction; Ethical Hacking Fundamentals.

Opportunities:
Opportunities are available to study at College as an Associate of Edinburgh Napier University. This opportunity may provide access to 3rd year of the following degree courses at Edinburgh Napier University:
BEng Computing
BSc IT Management
BEng Computer Security and Forensics
BEng Software Engineering

Entry:
HNC Computing
