Digital Media

Level of Qualification: Intermediate 2/Higher
SCQF Level: 5/6

This is a practical, hands-on course, suitable for school leavers or mature students who want to develop the key information technology and computing skills required to move onto a higher level computing or media course.

Duration:
One academic year. You will need to attend College for 18 hours of variable, timetabled study per week, plus commit to undertake further self study of at least 6 hours per week.

Aim:
You will gain knowledge and expertise in subjects such as animation fundamentals, software development, interactive multimedia for website development, computing games design, troubleshooting and securing IT systems.

Opportunities:
You will have the opportunity to complete the NPA in Computer Games Development.
You will have the chance to meet a variety of employers and learn more about potential job opportunities.

Entry:
Standard Grade passes in English, Mathematics and a Science subject at General level, or equivalent qualification.
Mature candidates with relevant experience will be considered.
